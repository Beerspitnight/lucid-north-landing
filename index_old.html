About Harry Llama

Harry Llama is the flagship solution from Lucid North. It's an AI-powered scheduler designed specifically for the unique complexities of K-12 schools. By automating substitute coverage, we turn hours of daily administrative work into minutes, freeing up school leaders to focus on students and staff.
