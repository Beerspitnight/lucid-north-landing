About Harry Llama

Harry Llama is the first flagship solution from Lucid North. It's an AI-powered substitute scheduler designed specifically for the unique complexities of K-12 schools. By automating substitute assignments, Harry eliminates hours of daily administrative work, prevents burnout, and provides principals with invaluable data-driven insights.
